Privacy Policy

ScrollWiz — Effective March 10, 2026

Overview

ScrollWiz is developed by Made By Pony LLC. Your privacy matters. This app is designed to keep all your data on your device and never transmit it anywhere.

Data Collection

ScrollWiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and is never sent to any server.

Accounts & Analytics

ScrollWiz does not require an account. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services integrated into the app.

FamilyControls

ScrollWiz uses Apple's FamilyControls framework for on-device app management only. No data from FamilyControls is collected, shared, or transmitted. All processing occurs entirely on your device.

Third Parties

ScrollWiz does not share data with any third parties. There are no ads, no SDKs, and no external services.

Children's Privacy

ScrollWiz is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
